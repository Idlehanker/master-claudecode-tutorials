# HF Papers Report — Top Notable Recent Papers (as of May 7, 2026)

*Note: HF's paper search ranks by relevance, not by weekly trending. The list below prioritizes papers from the past ~12 months with the highest community engagement (upvotes) and those most actively discussed.*

---

### 1. MLGym: A New Framework for Advancing AI Research Agents
**Upvotes:** 195 | **Date:** Feb 2025
A Meta-led framework and benchmark for evaluating LLM agents on 13 open-ended ML research tasks (CV, NLP, RL, game theory). Found that frontier models (Claude 3.5 Sonnet, GPT-4o, Llama 405B, Gemini 1.5 Pro) can improve baselines via hyperparameter tuning but cannot yet generate novel hypotheses or architectures.
— [Paper](https://hf.co/papers/2502.14499)

### 2. Speed Always Wins: Efficient Architectures for LLMs
**Upvotes:** 53 | **Date:** Aug 2025
A systematic survey covering linear/sparse attention, efficient full-attention variants, sparse MoE, hybrid architectures, and emerging diffusion LLMs.
— [Paper](https://hf.co/papers/2508.09834)

### 3. The AI Scientist-v2: Workshop-Level Automated Scientific Discovery
**Upvotes:** 22 | **Date:** Apr 2025
End-to-end agentic system that produced the first fully AI-generated paper accepted at a peer-reviewed ICLR workshop. Uses progressive agentic tree-search and a VLM feedback loop for figure refinement.
— [Paper](https://hf.co/papers/2504.08066)

### 4. Paper Espresso: From Paper Overload to Research Insight
**Upvotes:** 12 | **Date:** Apr 6, 2026
An open-source platform that auto-discovers, summarizes, and analyzes trending arXiv papers. Processes 13,300+ papers over 35 months. Revealed a mid-2025 surge in RL for LLM reasoning and 2x median upvotes for the most novel papers.
— [Paper](https://hf.co/papers/2604.04562)

### 5. Real-World Gaps in AI Governance Research
**Upvotes:** 12 | **Date:** Apr 30, 2025
Analysis of 1,178 safety papers across top AI companies and universities. Finds corporate AI research concentrates on pre-deployment (alignment, eval) while deployment-stage issues (bias, healthcare, finance, misinformation, copyright) have significant research gaps.
— [Paper](https://hf.co/papers/2505.00174)

### 6. HalluCitation: Hallucinated References in ACL Conferences
**Upvotes:** 7 | **Date:** Jan 26, 2026
Analysis of all ACL/NAACL/EMNLP papers from 2024–2025 reveals nearly 300 papers contain hallucinated citations. The problem is rapidly increasing — half were at EMNLP 2025.
— [Paper](https://hf.co/papers/2601.18724)

### 7. Why LLMs Aren't Scientists Yet
**Upvotes:** 6 | **Date:** Jan 6, 2026
Case study of four autonomous ML paper generation attempts using 6 LLM agents. Three failed; one was accepted. Documents six recurring failure modes including bias toward training data defaults, implementation drift, and weak scientific taste.
— [Paper](https://hf.co/papers/2601.03315)

### 8. APRIL: Efficient RL Training via Active Partial Rollouts
**Date:** Sep 23, 2025
Addresses the long-tail distribution problem in RL rollout generation for LLMs (GPT-o, DeepSeek-R1, etc.). Improves rollout throughput by 22.5% on average and achieves up to 8% higher final accuracy. GPU-agnostic.
— [Paper](https://hf.co/papers/2509.18521)

### 9. FOS: Temporal Graph Benchmark for Scientific Link Prediction
**Date:** Nov 23, 2025
Reconstructs 65K+ research sub-fields' co-occurrence graphs (1827–2024) as a temporal link-prediction benchmark for forecasting interdisciplinary breakthroughs.
— [Paper](https://hf.co/papers/2511.18631)

### 10. Deep Research of Deep Research: From Transformer to AI4Science
**Date:** Mar 30, 2026
Positions LLMs and Stable Diffusion as the twin pillars of generative AI, mapping a roadmap from Transformer → agents → AI for Science. Unifies industry deep research and academic AI4S perspectives.
— [Paper](https://hf.co/papers/2603.28361)

**Limitations:** The HF paper search indexes papers from arXiv but does not expose a "weekly trending" sort. Papers above are selected from those published in the last ~15 months with highest community signal (upvotes and discussion activity).
